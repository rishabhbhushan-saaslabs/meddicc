02
Metrics — Quantify the Opportunity
Put numbers on the pain
Metrics
▾
What KPIs matter most to you when calling prospects? (connect rate, conversion rate, average handle time, dials per rep)
MetricsALL
How many calls does an agent make per day on average? How many agents do you have in total?
MetricsLeader / Manager
Are your SDRs and AEs currently hitting quota? If not — what's the gap, and what do you attribute it to?
MetricsALL
If we improved your connect rate or conversion rate by X%, what would that mean for your revenue targets?
MetricsLeader / Manager
Force them to quantify business impact. This becomes your ROI anchor for the economic buyer conversation.
How important is speed-to-lead? How quickly do you need to reach a new inbound lead after they come in?

04
Decision Criteria — What "Right" Looks Like
Understand what they're scoring you against
Dec. Criteria
▾
What would a successful outcome look like — for you personally and for the team?
Dec. CriteriaALL
Do you have a preference on dialer type — Predictive, Power/Auto, Dynamic, or Preview? What's driving that preference?
Dec. CriteriaLeader / Ops
What CRM are you running? Do you need tight two-way sync — auto-logging calls, updating lists, launching campaigns from inside the CRM?
Dec. CriteriaOps / Leader
Is compliance a hard requirement? (10DLC, STIR/SHAKEN, CNAM, SOC 2, GDPR, call recording laws)
Dec. CriteriaLeader / Ops
Do you need local presence — displaying local area codes when calling across different regions?
Dec. CriteriaManager / Leader
Key for real estate, insurance, and high-volume outbound orgs.
Would AI coaching tools (call scoring, live script assist, moment analysis) be on your must-have list?
Dec. CriteriaManager / Rep
Would call summaries, action items, and auto-transcriptions be valuable for your workflow or coaching?

Decision Process — How They Buy
Map the buying journey before it maps you
Dec. Process
▾
Walk me through how a decision like this typically gets made at your company. Who else is involved?
Dec. ProcessALL
What's your timeline to make a decision? Is there an event or deadline driving that?
Dec. ProcessALL
Is scalability or security a concern? Will procurement need SOC 2 or GDPR documentation?
Dec. ProcessOps / Leader
What would need to be true for you to move forward in the next 30–60 days?
Dec. ProcessALL
Surfaces blockers early. Don't skip this — it's where deals die or accelerate.
06
Economic Buyer — Find the Power
Who controls the budget and the final yes?
Econ. Buyer
▾
Who ultimately signs off on a purchase like this? Is that person involved in our conversation today?
Econ. BuyerALL
Is budget already allocated for this, or would you need to build a business case internally?
Econ. BuyerLeader / Manager
No budget yet? Pivot to ROI framing. Use the metrics gap from Phase 2 to make the case.
If we can show strong ROI, could you get executive sponsorship? What would that internal conversation look like?
Econ. BuyerALL
What does the economic buyer care most about — cost reduction, revenue growth, compliance, or something else?
Econ. BuyerALL
Shape your demo and business case to hit their specific priority — not yours.
07
Competition — Know the Battlefield
Who else are they talking to?
Competition
▾
Are you evaluating other solutions right now? Who else are you looking at?
CompetitionALL
If they're hesitant: "Are you looking at dedicated dialers, or also all-in-one platforms?"
What have you seen from the other solutions you've evaluated? What's impressed you — and what's been disappointing?
CompetitionALL
Their answer tells you exactly what they'll use to compare you. Align your demo accordingly.
Is the status quo also on the table — meaning "do nothing" is still an option?
CompetitionALL
The status quo is always your biggest competitor. Make the cost of inaction explicit.
If you had to rank your top 3 criteria for choosing between solutions, what would they be?
CompetitionLeader / Manager
Directly reveals where you need to win. Use this to sharpen your close.
What would make you choose us over the other options you're looking at?
CompetitionALL
08
Champion — Build Your Internal Ally
Who fights for you when you're not in the room?
Champion
▾
How does this affect you personally? What does it mean for you if this problem finally gets solved?
ChampionALL
Champions need personal stake. Find their "why" — career visibility, team morale, their own quota.
How do you think a more efficient dialer would affect your team's motivation and daily energy?
ChampionManager / Rep
Who else in your org would benefit from seeing this? Would you be comfortable making an introduction?
ChampionALL
If we deliver on everything we've discussed, would you feel confident recommending this solution internally?
ChampionALL
Test champion strength. Hesitation = more discovery needed before advancing the deal.

Never leave without a concrete next step locked
Dec. Process Metrics
▾

Dec. ProcessALL
Always confirm your summary before proposing next steps. Misalignment here kills deals silently.

CompetitionALL
Can we schedule a deeper demo with [decision maker / champion] this week or next?
Dec. ProcessALL
What would you need to see in a demo or pilot to feel fully confident this is the right fit?
MetricsALL
Lock down success criteria now — not after the demo when it's too late to adjust.
